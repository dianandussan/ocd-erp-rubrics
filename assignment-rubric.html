Universal Assignment Rubric

Total Points: 100 points
Applies to: All assignments in the OCD/ERP Minicourse
Criteria Weight Excellent (4) Good (3) Satisfactory (2) Needs Improvement (1)
Content Mastery
Demonstration of course learning objectives 		35%
(35 points) 		Demonstrates comprehensive mastery of module learning objectives; uses course terminology with precision; shows deep understanding of concepts; accurately applies principles to scenarios Shows solid understanding of learning objectives; uses terminology correctly; demonstrates good grasp of concepts; adequately applies principles Shows basic understanding with some gaps; limited terminology use; demonstrates surface-level grasp; minimal application of principles Shows little understanding of objectives; incorrect terminology; significant misconceptions; no clear application
Critical Analysis
Higher-order thinking and problem-solving 		25%
(25 points) 		Provides sophisticated analysis; evaluates multiple perspectives; demonstrates complex problem-solving; shows original thinking and synthesis; makes insightful connections Shows adequate analysis; considers different viewpoints; demonstrates logical problem-solving; makes reasonable connections Limited analysis; basic problem-solving; few connections made; shows mostly concrete thinking No evidence of analysis; no problem-solving demonstrated; fails to make connections; purely descriptive
Organization & Structure
Logical flow and assignment requirements 		20%
(20 points) 		Exceptionally well-organized with clear structure; follows all assignment requirements precisely; logical flow of ideas; proper use of headings/sections; meets word count exactly Well-organized with good structure; follows most requirements; generally logical flow; appropriate sections; meets word count range Adequately organized; follows basic requirements; some organizational issues; meets minimum requirements Poor organization; fails to follow requirements; unclear structure; significantly under/over word count
Professional Presentation
Writing quality and formatting 		15%
(15 points) 		Outstanding writing quality; error-free grammar/spelling; professional formatting; appropriate tone for mental health content; engaging and clear communication Good writing quality with minor errors; proper formatting; appropriate tone; clear communication Adequate writing with some errors; basic formatting; generally appropriate tone; mostly clear Poor writing quality; numerous errors; improper formatting; inappropriate tone; unclear communication
Innovation & Creativity
Original thinking within academic framework 		5%
(5 points) 		Demonstrates exceptional creativity while maintaining academic rigor; original approaches to problem-solving; innovative connections; goes beyond basic requirements Shows good creativity within academic framework; some original thinking; makes interesting connections; meets requirements creatively Shows basic creativity; follows conventional approaches; minimal original thinking; meets requirements adequately Shows no creativity; purely formulaic approach; no original thinking; minimal effort
Performance Levels:
Excellent (4): 90-100% - Exceeds expectations
Good (3): 80-89% - Meets expectations
Satisfactory (2): 70-79% - Approaches expectations
Needs Improvement (1): Below 70% - Does not meet expectations

Scoring Calculation:
Total Score = (Content Mastery Score × 0.35) + (Critical Analysis Score × 0.25) + (Organization Score × 0.20) + (Professional Presentation Score × 0.15) + (Innovation Score × 0.05)
Multiply result by 25 to get final points out of 100
