Universal Discussion Rubric

Total Points: 50 points
Applies to: All discussion forums in the OCD/ERP Minicourse
Purpose: This rubric shows how each aspect of your discussion participation contributes to demonstrating your understanding and engagement with course content.

Criteria Weight Excellent (4)
90-100%		 Good (3)
80-89%		 Satisfactory (2)
70-79%		 Needs Improvement (1)
Below 70%
Content Knowledge
Understanding of course concepts 		40%
(20 points) 		Demonstrates comprehensive understanding of module concepts; uses course terminology accurately (obsessions, compulsions, ERP, exposure hierarchy); provides specific, relevant examples; clearly connects concepts to real-world applications Shows solid understanding of most concepts; uses terminology correctly with minor gaps; provides adequate examples; makes some connections to practical applications Shows basic understanding with some confusion; limited use of course terminology; provides general examples; minimal connections to applications Shows little understanding of concepts; incorrect or missing terminology; vague or irrelevant examples; no clear connections
Critical Thinking
Analysis and evaluation skills 		25%
(12.5 points) 		Provides thoughtful analysis of concepts; evaluates different approaches effectively; demonstrates insight into complexities; shows higher-order thinking and synthesis Shows adequate analysis with some depth; makes reasonable evaluations; demonstrates some insight; shows developing critical thinking Limited analysis or superficial thinking; basic evaluation with little depth; minimal insight; shows mostly concrete thinking No evidence of analysis; purely descriptive; no evaluation or insight; off-topic content
Participation
Engagement with peers and timeliness 		20%
(10 points) 		Posts initial response on time; provides 2+ meaningful peer responses; asks thoughtful questions; builds on others' ideas; maintains respectful tone throughout Posts on time; provides adequate peer responses; shows engagement with classmates; generally respectful and constructive Posts within grace period; basic peer responses; limited engagement; mostly appropriate tone Late posting; minimal or missing peer responses; superficial engagement; inappropriate tone
Writing Mechanics
Communication clarity and professionalism 		15%
(7.5 points) 		Clear, well-organized writing; proper grammar and spelling; meets word count requirements; uses professional tone appropriate for mental health topics Generally clear writing with minor errors; mostly organized; meets most requirements; appropriate tone Adequate writing with some clarity issues; multiple errors that don't impede understanding; partially meets requirements Poor writing quality that impedes understanding; numerous errors; fails to meet basic requirements

Performance Levels & Scoring

Excellent (4): 90-100% - Exceeds expectations and demonstrates mastery
Good (3): 80-89% - Meets expectations and shows solid understanding
Satisfactory (2): 70-79% - Approaches expectations with room for growth
Needs Improvement (1): Below 70% - Does not meet minimum standards

How Your Score is Calculated:
Total Score = (Content Knowledge Score × 0.40) + (Critical Thinking Score × 0.25) + (Participation Score × 0.20) + (Writing Mechanics Score × 0.15)
Multiply result by 12.5 to get final points out of 50

Minimum Passing: 35 points (70%)
